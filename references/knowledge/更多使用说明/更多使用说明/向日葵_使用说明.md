1.1 浏览器中访问 https://sunlogin.oray.com/download，点击个人版，选择Windows，下载“完整版64位”

## 二、Mac电脑


2.1 浏览器中访问 https://sunlogin.oray.com/download，点击个人版，选择macOS，下方点击立即下载，浏览器右上角可查看下载进度，下载完成后双击安装包


![](https://cofile.eeo.cn/res-store%2F2557dfcb4542070e380d9b8785251b75f000006b1155e6e9393eb771235d3a72_565040?q-sign-algorithm=sha1&q-ak=AKIDJQGsEOK2TfbFdEZifMnxrWx85mpdHj6H&q-sign-time=1777219200;1777824000&q-key-time=1777219200;1777824000&q-header-list=host&q-url-param-list=&q-signature=a44312a36ef2973d0e7980b001ebd4ab1eaf87cf)

![](https://cofile.eeo.cn/res-store%2F84f1c6762790d15e340d71af4d809aafbfc1f6cbc34df12a46d6203f4edf9e3f_1277894?q-sign-algorithm=sha1&q-ak=AKIDJQGsEOK2TfbFdEZifMnxrWx85mpdHj6H&q-sign-time=1777219200;1777824000&q-key-time=1777219200;1777824000&q-header-list=host&q-url-param-list=&q-signature=f62bdc018c3269c5b68ec5e8e85b61547d7e859c)
