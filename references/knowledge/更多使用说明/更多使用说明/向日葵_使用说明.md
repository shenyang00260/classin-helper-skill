## 二、Mac电脑


2.1 浏览器中访问 https://sunlogin.oray.com/download，点击个人版，选择macOS，下方点击立即下载，浏览器右上角可查看下载进度，下载完成后双击安装包

![](https://cofile.eeo.cn/res-store%2F84f1c6762790d15e340d71af4d809aafbfc1f6cbc34df12a46d6203f4edf9e3f_1277894?q-sign-algorithm=sha1&q-ak=AKIDJQGsEOK2TfbFdEZifMnxrWx85mpdHj6H&q-sign-time=1777824000;1778428800&q-key-time=1777824000;1778428800&q-header-list=host&q-url-param-list=&q-signature=9b93ee3df0ac761a3de877ef96928d6ca142b2da)
